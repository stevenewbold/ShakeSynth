# Privacy Policy for ShakeSynth

**Last updated: 05/01/2026**

## 1. Introduction
ShakeSynth (“the app”) is designed to operate entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. This policy explains our data practices in accordance with the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR) and the Data Protection Act 2018.

## 2. Data Controller
As ShakeSynth does not collect or process personal data, a data controller is not required. For support or questions, contact:

Email: steve@stevenewbold.co.uk

## 3. Data We Do Not Collect
ShakeSynth does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal or device information. Specifically, the app does not collect:

- Names, email addresses, or contact information  
- Photos, media, or files  
- Location data  
- Device identifiers  
- Usage statistics or analytics  
- Cookies or tracking data  
- Contact lists  
- Health, motion, or sensitive data (other than what is strictly required for core functionality)  
- Any information that could identify a user  

The app does not use accounts, does not require login, and does not communicate with external servers.

## 4. How the App Works
All functionality runs locally on the user's device.  
No data is stored outside the app environment and no data is transmitted off the device.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
ShakeSynth does not collect any personal information from users of any age, including children.

## 6. Data Sharing
ShakeSynth does not and cannot share any data because the app collects none.

## 7. Security
ShakeSynth does not store or process personal data. As a result, there is no risk of personal data exposure through external systems.

## 8. Your Rights Under UK GDPR
Because ShakeSynth does not collect, store, or process personal data, UK GDPR rights relating to personal data (access, correction, deletion, portability, etc.) do not apply.

## 9. Third-Party Services
ShakeSynth does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or data-processing services.

## 10. Changes to This Policy
If ShakeSynth’s functionality changes in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly. The “Last updated” date will reflect any changes.
